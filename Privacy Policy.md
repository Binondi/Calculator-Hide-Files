# Privacy Policy

Binondi built the **Calculator - Hide Files** app as an open-source application. This SERVICE is provided by Binondi at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform users regarding policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decides to use this Service.

---

## Information Collection and Use

Calculator - Hide Files is designed with privacy as a core principle.

The app **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information**. All files (including images, videos, audio, documents, and notes) are stored locally on your device.

The app does not require user registration, login, or account creation.

---

## Local Data Storage

All data processed by the app remains **on the user's device**. This includes:

* Hidden files (images, videos, audio, documents)
* Notes created within the app
* Encryption keys and security settings

The app does not upload or share this data with any external servers.

---

## Encryption and Security

The app provides features such as file encryption and passcode protection to help secure your data.

While reasonable measures are taken to protect your files, users are responsible for:

* Remembering their passcode or encryption key
* Safeguarding access to their device

Loss of the encryption key or passcode may result in permanent data loss, as the developer does not have access to user data.

---

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

Calculator - Hide Files does not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.

However, if the user chooses to use features such as **sharing files**, the selected file may be temporarily decrypted and shared with another application via Android’s standard sharing mechanism. Once shared, the data is subject to the receiving application's policies.

---

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

* **Storage / File Access**: Required to allow users to select, hide, and manage files on their device.

These permissions are used solely for the app’s core functionality and are not used for data collection.

---

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

If distributed through external platforms (such as app stores), those platforms may have their own data collection practices. Users are advised to review the respective privacy policies of those platforms.

---

## Children’s Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Since no personal data is collected, the app is generally safe for use by individuals of all ages. However, parental guidance is recommended when granting device permissions.
